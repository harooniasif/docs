How positive or negative is your brand portrayed on AI platforms. This is ranked out of 100, with a score below 50 meaning AI possesses a negative sentiment towards your brand.

## Why this matters:

* Surfaces potential reputation risks in AI platforms.  
* Tracks shifts in perception across regions, products, or industries.  
* Ensures your brand is not just visible but also framed positively.

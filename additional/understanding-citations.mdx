## Why Citation Analysis Matters

* It shows which domains drive your visibility, helps you understand your AI "footprint"  
* Reveals how your brand is framed by external sources across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, etc.  
* Identifies content and partnership opportunities based on where, when, and how your brand is cited  
* Helps you spot gaps where your competitors are being cited and you're not

## What You Can Do with It

* See top-level metrics (prompt mentions by domain, total links cited across platforms)  
* Dive into All Links Analysis, which URLs are cited, how often, and on which platforms  
* Explore Prompt Context, view the exact questions that led to citations, region data, and co-mentioned brands  
* Use insights for content strategy, competitive intelligence, and PR / partnerships

## Why It Helps You

* Focus your content on high-impact pages and domains  
* Close citation gaps where your competitors are winning  
* Discover authoritative partners or sources you should aim to be cited by  
* Monitor shifts in citation trends over time

## General Questions

### What is BrandRadar?

BrandRadar is a comprehensive Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) platform that helps businesses track and improve their visibility across AI platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude. It provides actionable insights to optimize your brand's presence in AI-generated answers.

### How is BrandRadar different from traditional SEO tools?

Unlike traditional SEO tools that focus on keyword rankings and search engine results, BrandRadar specializes in AI search optimization. It tracks how your brand appears in AI-generated answers, analyzes sentiment, monitors competitor positioning, and provides insights specific to the generative search landscape.

### Which AI platforms does BrandRadar monitor?

BrandRadar monitors your brand across major AI platforms including:
- ChatGPT (OpenAI)
- Google Gemini
- Perplexity
- Claude (Anthropic)
- And other leading AI platforms

## Getting Started

### How quickly can I see results?

You can get your first AI Visibility Report within minutes of setting up your brand. BrandRadar immediately starts collecting data and provides insights as soon as your configuration is complete.

### What information do I need to set up my brand?

To get started, you'll need:
- Your official brand name
- Your website domain
- Brand variations (nicknames, abbreviations)
- Geographic locations where you operate
- A brief description of what your brand does
- Your main competitors

### Can I add or modify my brand information later?

Yes, you can update your brand details, competitors, topics, and prompts at any time through the Data Builder interface. Changes will be reflected in your monitoring within minutes.

## Features & Functionality

### What is the Brand Visibility Score?

The Brand Visibility Score is your brand's total share-of-voice in the AI landscape. It shows how visible you are across AI platforms compared to competitors and provides a single benchmark to measure improvement over time.

### How does competitor benchmarking work?

BrandRadar automatically suggests major competitors based on your industry and brand description. You can add additional competitors and compare your visibility, topics, and prompt performance side-by-side to identify opportunities and gaps.

### Can I generate content with BrandRadar?

Yes, BrandRadar includes AI-ready content generation features. You can generate blog posts, landing pages, FAQ snippets, and other content optimized for AI platforms based on your performance insights.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes, all plans come with a 7-day free trial so you can explore BrandRadar's features before committing to a plan.

### Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account settings.

### What's the difference between the plans?

The main differences are:
- **Number of prompts** and **frequency** of monitoring
- **Monthly response limits** for data collection
- **Regional coverage** (Global only vs. multiple regions vs. unlimited)
- **Brand tracking limits** (1 to unlimited brands)
- **Content generation** capabilities (none to unlimited)
- **Team seats** (1 to unlimited)

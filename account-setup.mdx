# Brand Setup and Competitor Management

BrandRadar's insights depend on accurate brand and competitor information. Proper setup ensures the AI correctly identifies your brand mentions and filters out competitor noise. You can create new brands through the dashboard or edit existing settings under **Data builder → Brand details** and **Competitor details**.

<Info>
**Why This Matters:** Accurate brand and competitor setup is crucial for getting relevant insights. The AI uses this information to distinguish between your brand mentions and competitor activity.
</Info>

## Brand Creation Flow

After login, the platform displays a dashboard listing all brands in your company. Each brand card shows the brand's logo, description, brand visibility score gauge, number of prompts collected, and last update date.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Brand Dashboard">
    After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard with all your company's brands displayed as cards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Brand">
    Click the "Create a new brand" card to begin the brand creation process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Step 1">
    Enter the Brand name, Brand website, and alternative names/variations. The modal shows a progress bar indicating this is step 1 of 3.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Additional Steps">
    Follow the remaining steps to provide additional brand details and confirm creation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Brand Appears in Dashboard">
    After completing the onboarding, your new brand appears in the dashboard with View and Manage buttons for quick navigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Brand Cards:** Each brand card displays key information including logo, description, visibility score gauge, prompt count, and last update date. Use the View and Manage buttons for quick access to brand details.
</Note>

## 2.1 Editing Brand Details

Click the **Data builder** icon (connected nodes symbol) in the left navigation bar. A header appears with tabs for Prompts, Topics, Brand details, and Competitor details.

Select **Brand details**. You'll see fields for your Brand name, Website, Brand variations, and Operating locations. Fill these out to help the AI recognise mentions of your brand in different contexts.

Use the **What does your brand do?** and **What are you known for?** text boxes to describe your products or services. These descriptions refine the data the AI gathers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Data Builder">
    Click the Data builder icon in the left sidebar to access configuration options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Brand Details Tab">
    Choose the "Brand details" tab from the available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Brand Information">
    Complete all required fields with accurate information about your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click "Save changes" to store your brand configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Click **Save changes** to store your edits.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Brand Information Fields">
    **Essential Brand Details:**
    - **Brand Name**: Your primary brand name
    - **Website**: Your main website URL
    - **Brand Variations**: Common misspellings, abbreviations, and alternative names
    - **Operating Locations**: Geographic areas where you operate
    
    <Tip>Be comprehensive with brand variations - this helps our AI better identify and track your brand across platforms.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="pencil" title="Brand Descriptions">
    **Description Fields:**
    - **What does your brand do?**: Describe your products or services
    - **What are you known for?**: Key differentiators and brand attributes
    
    <Warning>These descriptions are crucial for AI understanding - be specific and comprehensive.</Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 2.2 Adding Competitor Brands

Still within **Data builder**, switch to the **Competitor details** tab. Here you'll find a table listing competitors with editable fields for their domains.

Enter each competitor's name and website. Accurate competitor details allow the AI to filter their mentions out of your brand analytics.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Adding Competitors">
    **Steps to Add Competitors:**
    1. Navigate to Data builder → Competitor details
    2. Click "Add Competitor" or similar button
    3. Enter competitor name and website
    4. Add any known brand variations
    5. Save the competitor information
    
    <Note>You can add or remove competitors at any time, but changes may take 24-48 hours to take effect.</Note>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Competitor Management">
    **Managing Competitor Data:**
    - Edit existing competitor information
    - Remove outdated competitors
    - Categorize competitors by market position
    - Set monitoring priority levels
    
    <Tip>Start with 3-5 key competitors and expand based on your monitoring needs.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

BrandRadar automatically collects and categorizes these prompts, then suggests the most relevant ones for you to review and select for analysis.

## Key things to know:

* The prompts and topics you choose define what conversations BrandRadar will track and analyze.  
* You can add or remove prompts anytime.  
* If you see low-quality results, refine your prompts or topics to better match your industry and goals.

## Next Steps:

* Review the suggested prompts.  
* Select the ones most relevant to your brand.  
* Click Complete when you're ready.

Within a minute, your first dashboard will be ready with live insights.
